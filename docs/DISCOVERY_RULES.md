# ROOTED Healthcare – Discovery Rules

## Authority

ROOTED Healthcare discovery is bound to the global ROOTED GEO law defined in:

rooted-core/docs/GEO_RULES.md

These rules CANNOT be overridden at the healthcare vertical level.

---

## Healthcare Discovery Behavior

All clinic, provider, program, and specialist discovery MUST obey:

- ✅ 50-mile maximum radius  
- ✅ 6–8 result limit per discovery row  
- ✅ Fair rotation across sessions  
- ✅ Local-only visibility by default  

This applies to:

- Primary care discovery  
- Specialist discovery  
- Mental health providers  
- Urgent care discovery  
- Public health programs  
- Community healthcare services  

---

## Filtering Rules

Healthcare filters MAY include:

- Specialty (cardiology, pediatrics, therapy, etc)
- Provider type (clinic, hospital, nonprofit, private)
- Program type (prevention, treatment, outreach)

Filters MAY NOT:

- Expand beyond 50 miles
- Exceed 8 results per row
- Force permanent placement
- Override global rotation

---

## Enforcement

Any violation of these rules is a platform-breaking defect and must be corrected at the core level.
